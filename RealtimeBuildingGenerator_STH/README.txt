RealtimeBuildingGenerator_STH
Bachelor's Diploma Project of Xuan Luo, Fudan University Under supervision of Professor Christopher Peters, KTH and Professor Rui Feng, Fudan University

Attempting to achieve a real-time building generator, focusing on a specific style - North German architecture style. Gamla Stan in Stockholm is a typical example of it.

Main methods that assigning attributes and choosing rules is based on "Instant Architecture", Wonka. Further attempts, components are being expanded.
